Chicken Fajita Wrap



How to make this delicious meal in short

For this you will need to cut some veggies into smaller pieces. For example, I use spring onion, white onion, paprika of any color and corn.
The spring onion should be cut in small pieces, the white onion and paprika into lines. The corn can be put straight from the can.

For the chicken I use clean breast and cut it also into small pieces. Spice it up with a fajita mix, fry it on the pan with some oil until it
tastes like it's done and put the veggies and some sauce of your choice. Continue frying for a little whilte.

Warm up the tortilla wraps in the device of your choice (I use the microwave or electril grill) and put the cooked ingredients inside.
Add some grated cheddar cheese or any kind of your choice on top and close it up. Enjoy your meal!


Ingredients

  • 400g of chicken breast
  • 1 white onion
  • 1/4 of spring onion
  • 1/3 of a can of corn
  • 3/4 of paprika
  • 200g of grated cheddar cheese
  • tortilla wraps

Step by step

  1. Cut the veggies into small pieces. Preferably, cut the white onion and paprika into lines
  2. Clean and cut the chicken breast into small pieces and mix it with a fajita mix
  3. Fry the chicken with some oil
  4. Add the vegetables to the pan and continue frying
  5. Add some sauce of your choice into the mix and continue frying for a little longer
  6. Heat up the tortilla wraps in the microwave or electric grill
  7. Add the ingredients inside of the wraps
  8. Add the grated cheese on top and close the wrap
  9. Enjoy your meal!
